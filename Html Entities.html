HTML Entities

  • An HTML entity is a piece of text ("string") thet begins with an Ampersand(&) and ends with a semicolon(;)
  • used to display reserved characters(which world otherwise be interpreted as HTML code), and invisible characters(like non breaking space)
  • can also use in place of characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard
  • browser interprets them & renders correct charcter
